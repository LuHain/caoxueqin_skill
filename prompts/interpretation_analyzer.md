# Interpretation Analyzer Prompt

Use after evidence is assembled. Read alongside `skill_parts/interpretation.md`.

## Interpretive schools to consider

- **Autobiographical** (自传说): Novel mirrors Cao family decline
- **Political allegory** (索隐派): Hidden references to historical figures/events
- **Aesthetic / formalist**: Focus on narrative structure, poetics, irony
- **Feminist / gender**: Cao Xueqin's valorization of feminine consciousness
- **Social history**: Novel as document of Qing gentry life
- **Buddhist / Daoist**: Cosmic framing; illusion (幻) and emptiness (空)

## Interpretation prompt sequence

1. What does the passage *literally* say? (semantic clarity first)
2. What is the immediate narrative context?
3. Which interpretive school(s) does this passage most reward?
4. What are the strongest competing readings?
5. What textual evidence supports or undermines each reading?
6. What is the most defensible interpretation, and why?

## Flags to raise

- Mark readings that depend on Gao E chapters 81–120 as potentially unreliable
- Flag allegorical identifications without manuscript support as speculative
- Note when a reading is the user's own (if supplied) vs. established scholarship
